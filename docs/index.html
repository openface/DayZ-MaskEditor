What it does

DayZ Mask Editor loads a terrain's layers.cfg, satellite map and surface mask, overlays the mask on the satmap, and lets you paint surfaces with a hard-edged pencil locked to the terrain's exact legend palette — then validates the result against DayZ's rules. It's a standalone rewrite of the GIMP "DayZ SatMask Tools" plugin, built to stay responsive on full-size (15360²) terrains.

  • Load layers.cfg, satmap and mask; overlay the mask with adjustable opacity.
  • Surface browser with live coverage %, texture thumbnails, and an eyedropper to arm a surface from the mask.
  • Hard pencil locked to legend colours, with a size-and-colour brush cursor and undo/redo.
  • Validation: stray-colour check and per-tile colour-budget check against Terrain Builder geometry.
  • Auto-fix: snap strays to the legend, and consolidate over-limit tiles.
  • Read-only shapefile overlays (Terrain Builder exports) to guide painting.
  • Cross-platform (Windows, macOS, Linux) with one-click install and delta auto-updates.
